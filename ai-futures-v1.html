This is a thinking tool, not a forecast. The directions each lever pushes reflect common themes in expert discussion about AI's effects. The math underneath is deliberately simple and transparent, so the tradeoffs stay visible rather than hidden behind false precision. The real point: notice that no lever is purely good, and that how we steer matters as much as how powerful the technology becomes.
